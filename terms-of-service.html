Description of Service

The App is a mobile application designed to help parents and guardians track their children's growth over time. The App provides:

  • Height, weight, and head circumference measurement recording and tracking for multiple children
  • Growth visualization through interactive charts for height, weight, and head circumference
  • Growth percentile comparisons using WHO and CDC reference data for height, weight, head circumference, and BMI
  • BMI (Body Mass Index) calculation by pairing height and weight measurements, with age-specific percentiles and category classification
  • Adult height predictions using growth percentile projection, mid-parental height calculation, and combined methods with confidence levels
  • Growth velocity analysis with monthly and annual growth rate tracking for height, weight, and head circumference
  • Height milestone detection and celebration with animated notifications
  • AR-based height measurement using device camera, LiDAR, and body pose detection
  • Interactive avatar that scales with your child's height and fun animal comparisons
  • Photo timeline, gallery with filtering, and full-screen viewing
  • Branded share cards for sharing growth milestones on social media
  • Sibling growth comparison charts for height, weight, and head circumference
  • PDF growth reports and CSV data export including BMI data
  • Local backup and restore of all app data (height, weight, head circumference, photos, and preferences)
  • iCloud Sync for syncing data across devices (Pro feature, optional)
  • Home Screen widgets (small, medium, and large) for quick growth summaries including BMI
  • Customizable measurement reminder notifications (weekly, monthly, or quarterly)
  • Multi-language support (English, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, and Chinese)
